How to Use

  1. Open a video on your favorite streaming site.
  2. Click the Duosub SRT icon in your browser toolbar.
  3. Drag and drop your .srt file or select it from your device.
  4. Adjust settings and enjoy dual-language learning!

Privacy Policy

Duosub SRT is built with privacy in mind. The extension processes subtitle files locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, browsing history, or the content of your subtitle files to external servers.
